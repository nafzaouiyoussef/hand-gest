How does it work?

  • Recognition Technology:

    Our system utilizes advanced gesture recognition technology powered by [insert relevant technology or algorithms] to interpret the intricate details of sign language gestures.
    The software is trained to recognize a wide array of signs, ensuring comprehensive coverage for effective communication.

  • Gesture Input

    Users can interact with HandSpeak by performing sign language gestures in front of a camera or using a dedicated input device.
    The system captures and analyzes these gestures in real-time, identifying the specific signs being expressed.

  • Translation to Text

    Once the signs are recognized, HandSpeak translates them into corresponding textual representations. This step ensures that the intended message is accurately captured and ready for further processing.

  • Speech Synthesis

    The translated text is then converted into natural and intelligible speech using state-of-the-art speech synthesis technology.
    Users have the option to customize the voice settings, allowing for a personalized and comfortable listening experience.
